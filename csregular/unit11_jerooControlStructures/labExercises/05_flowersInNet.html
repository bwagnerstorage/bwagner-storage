Flowers In Net

Directions

Create a jeroo at (5,0), facing east, with 10 flowers in its pouch.

The jeroo should travel from the east shoreline to the west shoreline. Whenever the jeroo encounters a complete horse-shoe shape made out of nets to its left, the jeroo should place a flower in the opening.

The number of nets and the spacing between the nets is unknown. Your code should work no matter how the nets are arranged in rows 3 and 4.

Method turnAround

Write a method named turnAround as described by its pre and post conditions below. Use this method in your implementation of the program.

// postcondition: The Jeroo has turned around and now is facing in the opposite direction.

}
