MineSweeper

Directions

A jeroo with 50 flowers in its pouch travels across an island one time from West to East disabling every net it finds in its path and replacing the net with a flower (first, toss a flower at the net to disable it, then hop forward and plant another flower to replace the net). The program should use a loop to continuously find and replace nets until the shore is reached. Your implementation should work for any island and from any starting location on the East shoreline.

Test your jeroo at the following locations: (0,0), (4,0), and (10,0).

Hint: You will need to use an if/else statement in your solution.
