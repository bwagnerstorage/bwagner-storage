Grade Needed

Directions

Write a program that given a student's three six weeks averages will calculate the grade the student needs to make on his/her semester exam to earn the desired semester average.

For example, Zack wants to earn at least a 90 for the semester. If his three six weeks averages are 92, 86, and 88 he would need to make at least a 95 on his semester exam to achieve his goal.

The table below shows other examples.

Goal Six Weeks Avgs Grade Needed
80 86,78,62 98.7
70 75,65,60 83.3
90 99,100,99 52.7

Here is the formula for calculating the grade needed on the semester exam:

gradeNeeded = (semesterAvgGoal - (0.8 * (sixWeeks1 + sixWeeks2 + sixWeeks3) / 3)) / 0.2
